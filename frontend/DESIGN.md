# TutorFinder Design System

This design system embodies **Vibrant Academic Professionalism**. It is tailored for the educational landscape of Nepal, blending traditional trust with modern SaaS efficiency. The aesthetic is defined by "High-Contrast Functionalism"—a style that rejects soft gradients and fuzzy shadows in favor of crisp, decisive lines and intentional offsets.

## Brand & Style
The brand personality is authoritative yet energetic. By utilizing a "Zero-Blur" shadow philosophy, the UI gains a 2.5D tactile quality that feels structured and dependable.

## Colors
The palette is rooted in **Vibrant Leaf Green (#83B822)**, symbolizing growth and the academic "green light" for success. **Deep Royal Purple (#5722B8)** serves as the sophisticated accent.

- **Primary:** #83B822 (Vibrant Leaf Green)
- **Secondary:** #5722B8 (Deep Royal Purple)
- **Text:** #141416 (Charcoal)
- **Background:** #FFFFFF / #fcf8fb
- **Tertiary:** #F4F7ED (Soft Green Tint)

## Typography
- **Primary Font:** Roboto Flex
- **Body Large:** 18px
- **Body Medium:** 16px
- **Headlines:** Tighter letter spacing, heavier weights.

## Elevation & Depth
- **Zero-Blur Hard Offsets:** Instead of soft shadows, use a flat color block offset.
- **Primary Elevation:** 10px horizontal and 10px vertical offset using `rgba(131, 184, 34, 0.15)`.
- **Interactive State:** Offset reduces to 4px on hover.

## Shapes
- **Buttons & Search Inputs:** Full Pill Radii (rounded-full).
- **Cards:** rounded-xl (24px).

## Layout
- **Grid:** 4px grid system.
- **Max Container:** 1280px.
- **Vertical Spacing:** 80px (xl) between major sections.
